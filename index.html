    🌱 Help & FAQ

    How many worksheets can I generate?
    It depends on your plan: Free = 5/month, Parent = 20/month, Family Plus = 60/month, BrightBridge Pro = 150/month. Your count resets on your personal renewal date, shown at the top of the page.
    What's the difference between Printable and Interactive mode?
    Printable mode creates a worksheet you can download or print at home. Interactive mode turns it into an on-screen quiz your child can answer directly, with optional read-aloud support.
    What is Dysgraphia-Friendly Mode?
    It reformats the worksheet to reduce handwriting — using checkboxes, word banks, and larger spacing — so your child is tested on understanding, not handwriting endurance. You can turn it on for any subject or grade.
    Are the worksheets aligned with DepEd's curriculum?
    Yes. Topics follow the DepEd MATATAG curriculum guide, organized by grade level and quarter, for Grades 1–6.
    I used all my free worksheets. What now?
    You can wait for your monthly allowance to renew, or upgrade to a paid plan for more worksheets. Just message us and we'll help you get set up.
    Can I use this for more than one child?
    Yes! Family Plus is designed for households with multiple children, with a higher monthly worksheet allowance.
